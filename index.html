About Me

Hi! I'm Novia, and I believe the most exciting moment in data science is when a model leaves the notebook and starts making real decisions in the real world. That bridge between research and production is where I thrive.

I'm a Data Scientist with 2.5+ years of experience across NLP, machine learning, MLOps, and large-scale data engineering. Most recently at Amazon Prime Video, I built multilingual NLP pipelines processing 1.5M+ catalog titles, architected graph systems connecting 404K+ entities, and optimized NER models across 100+ languages. Before that, I spent nearly two years at Accenture scaling 30+ production ETL pipelines and earning the ACE Award for boosting data throughput by 85%.

Currently pursuing my MS in Data Analytics Engineering at Northeastern University with a 4.0 GPA. I presented my Financial Stress Test Platform at Google's Cambridge office during MLOps Innovation Day — compressing two days of portfolio stress testing into two minutes.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring how AI intersects with finance and healthcare, tinkering with end-to-end deployments, and finding elegant solutions to messy data problems.
